About Us

Welcome to Horizon Path Travel, where your journey begins before you even pack your bags. Whether you’re traveling solo, with family, or as part of a group, we’re here to turn your travel dreams into reality. At Horizon Path Travel, we don’t just plan trips — we craft unforgettable stories.
